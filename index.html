How I started to code?


Hi everyone, I'm a girl who loves coding ever since my brother introduced me to a new language called Ruby! It wasn't a great experience at first because everything seemed so dull and it didn't capture my full attention, so I slept on it. Regardless, the curiousity about coding still lingered in me, and I finally decided to take it seriously by pushing my own limits, studying how to code through Codecademy during my A-level days. However, progress was REAL SLOW. Without further delay, after A-levels, I entered a full stack Web Development Bootcamp at NEXT Academy and realized my passion towards the whole tech industry. Everthing I did and done was a great satisfication which makes me want to step further and deeper into it. Never once was I bored about learning something new about coding; unlike the many things I ended up quitting, this was very different. I wanted to do well and I was always eager to learn it, from anyone and anywhere! Hence, I joined many tech events(Techladies, WWCode, RailsGirls, Google events, etc) and it's great because that's when and where many like minded people would gather and talk about their ideas. Last but not least, it's only the beginning for me; I still have a long way to go, to gain more experiences and connections, so hire me today!
